About Us

The magnificent villas of Porto Zante Villas & Spa offer a wonderful living environment: Antique marble is on the spotlight, while stunning open living areas, with immense windows and private terraces bathe the sitting room and bedrooms in the sunlight.
Everything a guest might need can be found in the villas, from a personal working desk with iMac desktop and complimentary Wi-Fi internet, to a Sony Playstation 4 and satellite TV in all rooms and bathrooms. Guests can relax in their private garden, or enjoy dinner at their private Gervasoni outdoor lounge.
